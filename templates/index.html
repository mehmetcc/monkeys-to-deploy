Monkey Classifier

What is Monkey Classifier?

Monkey Classifier is a ResNet50 trained with bunch of images of monkeys.

There are 10 classes of different monkey species it can predict with almost 100% accuracy.

Why Should I use it?

Because no matter what kind of image you upload, the neural network would still try to produce a prediction.

That means that you can actually find which monkey species your friend resemble, and that is kinda fun, so...
